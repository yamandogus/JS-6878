London

London is the capital of England.
It is the most populous city inthe United Kingdom, with a metropolitan area of over 9 million.

Paris

Paris is the capital and most populous city of France.
Located on the Seine River, it has a population of over 2 million within its city limits.

Tokyo

Tokyo is the capital of Japan and the most populous metropolitan area in the world.
With over 37 million people, it is also a global financial center.
